# Sleep Pattern & Lifestyle Analysis ( Excel Project )

## Overview
This project analyzes sleep patterns and daily lifestyle habits to understand their impact on tiredness levels. 
Using Excel, raw survey data was cleaned, categorized, and transformed into an interactive dashboard that highlights.
Key insights related to sleep duration, phone usage, and breakfast habits.
The project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis using Excel — from raw data to actionable insights.

## Objectives
- Analyze average sleep duration and tiredness levels.
- Understand how insufficient sleep affects fatigue.
- Study the impact of phone usage on tiredness.
- Examine the relationship between breakfast habits and energy levels.
- Present insights through a professional Excel dashboard.

## Dataset
- Source: sleep_data.csv
- Records: Survey based lifestyle and sleep data

## Key Fields:
- Sleep_Hours  
- Enough_Sleep (Yes/No)  
- Tired_Level (1–5 scale)  
- Phone_Usage  
- Phone_Reach  
- Breakfast

## Derived Field:
- Sleep_Group: (<= 5 hours, 6 -7 hours, >= 8 hours)

## Tools Used:
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Excel formulas for data cleaning and categorization
- Dashboard design and KPI reporting

## Key Insights
- Average sleep duration is 6.66 hours, below recommended levels.
- Average tiredness level is 3.09 / 5, indicating moderate fatigue.
- 66% of individuals report not getting enough sleep.
- Shorter sleep duration correlates with higher tiredness.
- Excessive phone usage is associated with increased fatigue.
- Individuals who skip breakfast tend to feel more tired.

## Business Takeways:
- Promoting 7–8 hours of sleep can significantly reduce fatigue.
- Reducing late-night phone usage may improve sleep quality.
- Healthy morning routines, including breakfast, support better energy levels.
- Lifestyle factors should be analyzed together, not in isolation.

## Conclusion:
- The analysis shows that insufficient sleep is widespread and strongly linkedto higher tiredness levels. 
- Lifestyle behaviors such as phone usage and skipping breakfast further contribute to fatigue. 
- Improving sleep duration alongside healthier daily habits can significantly enhance energy, focus, and productivity.
